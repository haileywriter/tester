About Me

Hello everyone!

My name is Hailey Gonzales.

I love to write about complex characters. I love flawed characters who are just trying their best. Found family is my absolute favorite trope and no matter how hard I try, I can't stay away from writing angst. It's my favorite thing to read, and it's something I love to include.

I completed my first novel novel in sixth grade after my teacher told us we could write a story like R.L. Stine. What she meant was a short story but there were no takesies backsies! I ended up writing a 200 pages story with a 200 page sequel. It was horrible and terribly written, but I am still so proud of it. That novel kickstarted my writing career.

I wrote the first Jensen book in high school and wrote the series on and off for years. I majored in Computer Science at Sacramento State and graduated Fall 2021. When I'm not writing, I'm a substitute teacher.

I'm a total nerd. I joined the anime club in college. My room is plastered in anime posters and I love to collect anime figurines. Reading fanfiction is one of guiltiest pleasures, but I highly recommend it! You can find the perfect fic just by searching your favorite tropes!

I have a dog named Miley who I absolutely adore. She is the cutest animal in the multiverse. She's part Pomeranian, part Chihuahua and she's been my writing buddy ever since she was born.

I hope you enjoy your visist here! Thanks for making it to the end!
